About us

Artcool project is an opportunity to make the art world more accessible.

To surround yourself with the masterpieces of world painting, you don't need to be a multimillionaire. Just the desire and a little time spent to choose a painting to your liking is enough.

We, the «Artcool» company, help to get acquainted with art and sell paintings, our goal is to develop creativity and teach art, we also advise and deliver around the city completely free of charge. We help to create creative and beautiful portfolios for you. We will be happy to help you!

Thank you for choosing us!
